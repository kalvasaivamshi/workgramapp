What is Workgram?

A application that stands as a platform between enthusiastic learners and trainers of various domains conducting freeworkshops.

A learner can see all the details provided by trainers which enables them to connect and clarify their issues personally.

No tension of missing the registration date.

Wondering how ? Because we don't have any.

Interested in a workshop? Join the link on the scheduled time to dive right in.

Any user can provide their valuable feedback on the workshop and it is enabled in such a way that these comments are only visible to users like him.

Staying in different town, don't have free trainers in your state?

Don't wotrry the workshops are all conducted on gmeet enabling the users from anywhere join their favourite workshops.

Our Story

Reverting back to Who are we... Four friends from college who wondered why a trainer who is teaching out for free has go class to class to tell about the workshop and give the details to interested students.

They decided this is not how free training camps should be treated as. They need not roam around to give out something valuable for free.

Today we are here giving our idea into life.
